Intended Use and Limitations
============================

This artifact is released for academic research and reproducibility evaluation
purposes only.

- It contains source code and sample datasets derived from public IoT traffic
  collections.
- The provided PCAPs are small anonymized examples and do not contain sensitive
  information.
- The method is designed for smart home device identification in research
  settings; it is not optimized for commercial deployment or large-scale
  monitoring without further engineering work.

Users may modify and extend the code under the terms of the included license
(see license.txt).
